Cartesian Bio is an early-stage (pre-funding) startup. We are building a precision medicine tool that helps patients who have a known disease, for which either (1) there is no known treatment, or (2) all the known treatments have been tried but are not effective.

Starting with a molecular profile of a tissue sample from the patient, we create a data-driven analysis of available therapies. The result is a report that can guide the patient's physician to find a safe and effective FDA-approved therapy.

Check out our buzzword score:

  • patient-centric - our focus is on the needs of patients, to get them the best therapy possible
  • genomics - it all starts with the patient's RNA
  • biomedical informatics - we crunch data to make better use of existing drugs, we are not inventing new ones
  • precision medicine - we use a patient's genetic expression data to recommend targeted therapies
  • big data - we use predictive algorithms to match patients to appropriate therapies

    • Cartesian Bio is a spinoff from the Department of Biomedical Informatics at Harvard Medical School. We are based in Boston, MA.
